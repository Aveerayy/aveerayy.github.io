Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect it.

🔒 Our Promise

HabiTrait is built with privacy at its core. We believe your personal development journey should be exactly that—personal. Your habit data, personality insights, and progress are yours alone.

🌟 The short version: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information. Period.

📱 Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure frameworks:

  • Habits and completions — Stored on-device only
  • Personality goals — Stored on-device only
  • Analytics and progress — Calculated on-device only
  • Settings and preferences — Stored on-device only

We use Apple's SwiftData framework for secure, local data persistence. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

🚫 What We Don't Collect

HabiTrait does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

🔐 Security Features

We've implemented additional security measures to protect your data:

  • Face ID / Touch ID — Optional biometric protection for app access
  • On-device encryption — Your data is encrypted using iOS security standards
  • No cloud sync — Data remains exclusively on your device

📤 Data Export & Sharing

You have full control over your data:

  • Export — You can export your habit data at any time through the app settings
  • Share — You can share progress screenshots or summaries—this is always your choice
  • Delete — Deleting the app removes all data permanently

🔔 Notifications

If you enable reminders, HabiTrait uses Apple's local notification system. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and are not routed through any external servers.

👶 Children's Privacy

HabiTrait does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in personal development.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
